AdLens Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 4, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to AdLens ("we", "our", or "the App"). AdLens is an application that allows you to monitor your AdMob advertising revenue simply and quickly. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what your rights are.

By using AdLens, you accept the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Data Collected

1. Google Access Data

AdLens uses Google authentication (OAuth 2.0) to access your AdMob data. When you sign in, we collect:

  • Google User ID: To identify your account
  • Access Token: To access AdMob APIs on your behalf
Important: We do not store your Google credentials. Access tokens are used only to retrieve data from AdMob during the active session.

2. AdMob Data

AdLens accesses the following data from your AdMob account:

  • Performance data: Estimated earnings, impressions, clicks, eCPM, CTR
  • Geographic data: Country of traffic origin
  • Temporal data: Revenue trends over time
  • App information: App name, platform (Android/iOS)

3. Local Data

AdLens stores locally on your device:

  • Cached data: To improve app performance
  • Preferences: Your chosen settings
  • Session history: Only to keep the session active

This data is not transmitted to our servers and remains only on your device.

How We Use Your Data

The collected data is used exclusively for:

  1. Display your revenue: Show you your AdMob account performance data
  2. Aggregate analysis: Create charts and statistics on your revenue
  3. Improve experience: Optimize the app interface and features
  4. Debug and troubleshoot: Fix any errors in the app

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Your AdMob data is used exclusively within the app and is not shared with any other service, except:

  • Google AdMob: Data comes from official Google APIs
  • App analytics services: In anonymized form to improve the app (optional, can be disabled)

Data Security

We employ appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: Data in transit is protected with HTTPS
  • No remote storage: Your sensitive data is never saved on our servers
  • Temporary tokens: Access tokens are used only during the session
  • Secure authentication: We use Google's OAuth 2.0 for sign-in

Data Retention

  • Local data: Remains on your device until you clear cache or uninstall the app
  • Access tokens: Revoked when you sign out of the app
  • Anonymized analytics: May be retained to improve the app

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  1. Access: You can request information about the data we process
  2. Deletion: You can request removal of your data
  3. Portability: You can request a copy of your data
  4. Withdraw consent: You can revoke access at any time

Children

AdLens is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
